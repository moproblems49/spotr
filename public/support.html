Report a problem or abusive content

You can report objectionable content or an abusive user directly in the app: tap the ··· menu on any post, profile, group post, or direct message and choose Report. You can also block a user from their profile. We review reports and act on objectionable content and abusive users within 24 hours. To report something urgent, email us at the address above.

Delete your account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app: Profile → Settings → Delete account. If you can't access the app, email us and we'll complete the deletion within 30 days.

Common questions

  • My workouts didn't sync. Seshd saves every workout on your device first and syncs to the server when you're back online — nothing is lost in a gym dead zone. Reopen the app with a connection and it will sync automatically.
  • Apple Health data looks off. Readiness and recovery use HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep from Apple Health. Grant access when prompted (or in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health), and allow a day or two of data for estimates to settle.
  • I forgot my password. On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? to get a reset link by email (check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive).
  • Group photos. Photos posted in a group are private to that group's members and aren't visible on the public feed.
