Seshd Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Seshd ("the app", "we") is a workout tracking and fitness social app. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We do not sell your data, and we do not show ads.

Information you provide

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name. Authentication is handled by Supabase, our backend provider.
  • Workout data. Exercises, sets, reps, weights, RPE ratings, and workout timestamps you log are stored in your account so they sync across sessions and power features like progress tracking, muscle readiness, and strength analysis.
  • Profile and social content. Profile details, posts, and workout recaps you choose to share are stored and visible to other users according to the sharing options you select.

Health data (Apple HealthKit)

With your permission, Seshd reads heart rate variability (HRV) and resting heart rate from Apple Health. This data is used solely on your device to estimate daily recovery and muscle readiness against your personal baseline.

  • HealthKit data is processed on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising, marketing, or sold to third parties, and is never disclosed to data brokers.
  • You can revoke access at any time in the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security → Health, and the app will continue to work without it.

Information collected automatically

The app stores preferences and cached data locally on your device to function offline and load faster. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or tracking across other companies' apps and websites.

How your data is used

  • To provide core app functionality: logging workouts, computing readiness and strength insights, and displaying your history.
  • To operate social features: showing posts you choose to share to other users.
  • To maintain and secure your account.

Data sharing

We share data only with the service providers required to run the app: Supabase (database, authentication, and storage) and Vercel (web hosting). These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes. We do not sell personal data and do not share it with advertisers.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below, and we will complete the deletion within 30 days.

Children

Seshd is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.
