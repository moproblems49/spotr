SESHD — iOS SETUP NOTES (for the Mac / Xcode side)
====================================================

1) Info.plist — add these keys (ios/App/App/Info.plist):
---------------------------------------------------------
<key>NSHealthShareUsageDescription</key>
<string>Seshd reads your heart rate variability and resting heart rate to estimate daily recovery and muscle readiness. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded or shared.</string>

(No NSHealthUpdateUsageDescription needed — the app only reads, never writes.
Don't add it; declaring write access you don't use can trigger review questions.)

2) PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy
---------------------------------------------------------
- Place the provided PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy file at: ios/App/App/PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy
- In Xcode: right-click the "App" group → "Add Files to App…" → select it →
  make sure "App" target is checked. It must be a member of the app target
  or it does nothing.

3) Capabilities (Xcode → App target → Signing & Capabilities)
---------------------------------------------------------
- Add capability: HealthKit (no background delivery / clinical records needed)
- This also needs HealthKit enabled on the App ID in the Apple Developer
  portal — Xcode usually does this automatically with automatic signing.

4) App Store Connect — App Privacy questionnaire (answers that match
   the privacy manifest + policy):
---------------------------------------------------------
- Data collected: Email Address, Name, Fitness (workout data),
  Other User Content (posts/photos) — all "Linked to user",
  all "App Functionality", none used for tracking.
- Health data (HRV/resting HR): NOT collected — it never leaves the device.
  Do NOT check "Health" in the questionnaire; checking it implies it's
  transmitted off-device.
- Tracking: No.
- Privacy Policy URL: https://spotr-drab.vercel.app/privacy.html
  (or your custom domain once you have one)

5) Misc
---------------------------------------------------------

  pulling, then build/archive as usual.
